Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Budget Studio is a personal budgeting app operated by an individual (not a company). This page explains what data is stored and how you can control it. It is not a substitute for legal advice.

What we store

If you create an account, we store your email, optional display name, and the budget data you enter (categories, amounts, notes, schedules) in Supabase (hosted Postgres + Auth). On this device the browser may also keep a local cache so the app works offline.

What we do not collect

We do not ask for bank logins, account numbers, Social Security numbers, or payment card details. There is no bank aggregation or advertising tracker in this app.

How data is protected

Passwords are handled by Supabase Auth (hashed; we never see plaintext passwords). Budget rows use Row Level Security so a signed-in user can only access their own data. Cloud data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest by the hosting providers.

Who can see your data

Only you (via your account) and the app operator with privileged Supabase access for maintenance. We do not sell personal data.

Your choices

  • Export CSV or JSON from Settings anytime.
  • Delete cloud budget data from Settings → Delete cloud data (then sign out).
  • To remove your Auth login entirely, ask the operator (email below) or delete the user in the Supabase dashboard (Authentication → Users) if you have project access.

Disclaimer

Budget Studio is for personal organization only. It is not financial, legal, tax, or investment advice.
