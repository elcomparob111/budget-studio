## Disclaimer

- Using a name in an app does **not** create a registered trademark.
- Descriptive phrases (“Budget” + “Studio”) may be harder to register and easier to collide with.
- No USPTO, EUIPO, WIPO, App Store, or common-law search has been performed as part of this audit.
- Do not claim “®” or “™ registered” until counsel confirms registration status.

## Recommended clearance steps (before commercial scale)

1. **USPTO TESS** (and state databases if relevant) search for “Budget Studio” and close variants.
2. **App Store / Google Play** search for identical or confusingly similar titles.
3. **Domain / social** handle availability (`budgetstudio`, etc.) — defensive registration if commercializing.
4. **Counsel** — short clearance memo if raising capital, selling, or national advertising.
5. If conflict found: rename early (cheaper than after App Store reviews and marketing).
